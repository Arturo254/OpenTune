A stylish YouTube Music client with Material Design 3 for Android

<br><b>Features:</b>

- 🎵 Ad-free YouTube music and YouTube Music playback.
- 🔊 Background playback.
- 🔍 Advanced search for songs, videos, albums, and playlists.
- 🔑 Integrated login.
- 📚 Full management of your music library.
- ⬇️ Downloading and caching for offline playback.
- 🗣️ Synchronized lyrics.
- ⏩ Smart silence skipping.
- 🔊 Audio normalization.
- ⏱️ Tempo and pitch adjustment.
- 🌗 Dynamic theme.
- 🌍 Multi-language support.
- 🚗 Android Auto support.
- 💎 Material Design 3.
- 🔗 Album art download
